Gaining in massive popularity and putting his staple on the Caribbean music scene as the rap sensation of the year on the ABC islands and the Netherlands is the Curaçaoan rap artist Dongo. Born Rachendrik Esthanista(1988) in Curaçao, the origin of his artist name lies with a cartoon show his brother used to watch as a kid.
